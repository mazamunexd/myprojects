Academic Programs

Quality and relevant education that responds to the call of the present times in building the foundations of the future.

The University provides accessible education from high school to doctoral courses, through both traditional and nontraditional systems. We continuously enrich our academic programs to equip students with employable, managerial, and entrepreneurial skills.

Our Commitments

Quality

We maintain high standards of excellence in education.

Relevance

Our programs equip students with competitive and essential skills.

Flexibility

We offer diverse learning methods including classroom, home, and online education.

Accreditation

Our courses are accredited by AACCUP.

Universal Access to Quality Tertiary Education Act

We provide free tuition and exemption from other fees as a state university.
